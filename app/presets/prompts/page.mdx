## Custom Prompts

In addition to built-in identifiers, you can define your own prompts.

A custom prompt is created by specifying a <code className="whitespace-break-spaces text-sm rounded-md bg-gray-800 px-1 py-0.5">role</code> and a <code className="whitespace-break-spaces text-sm rounded-md bg-gray-800 px-1 py-0.5">content</code> field
